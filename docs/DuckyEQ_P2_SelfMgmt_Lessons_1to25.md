**5.** *\[surprised\] \"Brain fact: breathing OUT longer than you

key --- every time.\"*

**6.** *\[happy\] \"All three work. The best one is whichever one you
will actually remember to use. Pick one. Learn it. Use it.\"*

**7.** *\[encouraging\] \"You can do any of these anywhere --- in class,
